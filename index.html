Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will be adding specific points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number. You lose the game if your score goes above that number.

The value of the crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts the game will change the value of each crystal.
